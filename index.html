The Advisory

Karyn Lovegrove Art Advisory works with our clients to build exceptional art collections. Our particular expertise focuses on the most important modern and contemporary art. The core principle of our business is to create tailor made collections that reflect the diverse interests of our clients while always maintaining an eye toward building a sound group of investments.

Access

We specialize in researching and identifying opportunities for investment in the art market and have unparalleled access to make acquisitions. The work that we recommend is sourced from a wide network of local and international galleries, private dealers and collectors, auction houses and art fairs. These relationships have been established over a career in art advising and consulting spanning 20 years, allowing us to present our clients with the best works by the finest artists at all career levels; from emerging, to mid-career, to established. It is our skill at selecting artists whose work will stand the test of time, and to identify the very best works by those artists, that give us the rare ability to advise clients in making the informed choices necessary to create special and valuable art collections. There is a vast spectrum of choices in the art world and it is an extraordinary benefit to have the professional assistance and recommendations of a specialist in navigating this uniquely complicated business.

Residential and Commercial Partnerships

We have particular expertise in working with interior designers and architects on residential and commercial projects of all scales. Sourcing and commissioning artworks, for both interior and outdoor spaces, is a focus of our practice.

Collection Management

We offer a full range of art advisory and collection management services, including acquisitions and re-sales, specialized advice on estate planning and family trusts, advice on 1031 exchanges, framing consultation, specialized art installation, art crating, shipping and storage services, market, insurance and charitable gift donation valuations, art education, photography, and catalogue production and publication.

We are fortunate to work with a select group of collectors, ranging from families with major, institution worthy holdings built by us over the span of many years, to individuals and corporations focusing on specific design projects. We ourselves are passionate about art and artists, and we enjoy encouraging and fostering in our clients that same sense of passion for discovery, and in growing a burgeoning collection into a highly cultivated and valuable portfolio of artworks.

We pride ourselves on the fact that we have built long-term relationships with our collectors by providing them with extraordinary service and by helping them to make acquisitions that are both much loved and very financially rewarding. Discretion and privacy are of the utmost importance in our business, and our clients appreciate our vigilance on their behalf.
